# Recipe: Egg-Free Huevos Rancheros
tags: breakfast, lunch
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 tortille
- [ ] Fasola czarna (smazona z mlekiem i kuminem)
- [ ] Tofu (scramble)
- [ ] Sos Ranchero: Passata, jalapeno, kumin, oregano
- [ ] Awokado, kolendra, limonka

## Steps
1. Ugotuj sos ranchero
2. Poddus fasole (zgniec). Zrob tofu scramble
3. Naloz na tortille: fasole, tofu, sos i dodatki
