# Recipe: Carrot Soup with Tempeh
tags: soup, dinner
time: 40 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Carrots, onion, garlic, sweet potato
- [ ] Cornstarch (for thickening)
- [ ] Broth, plant-based milk
- [ ] Tempeh (fried with soy sauce and paprika)
- [ ] Pickled onion (from a jar)

## Steps
1. Cook and blend the soup. Serve with roasted sweet potato and fried tempeh
