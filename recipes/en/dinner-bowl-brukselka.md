# Recipe: Dinner Bowl with Brussels Sprouts
tags: dinner
time: 35 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Brussels sprouts (roasted)
- [ ] Buckwheat or quinoa
- [ ] Avocado, carrot, edamame
- [ ] Tahini-lemon sauce

## Steps
1. Roast Brussels sprouts 25 min at 200C. Cook the grain
2. Arrange everything in a bowl. Drizzle with sauce
