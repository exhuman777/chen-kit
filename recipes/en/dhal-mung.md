# Recipe: Mung Dhal (Ayurveda)
tags: dinner, ayurveda
time: 45 min
source: PDF ayurvedic

## Ingredients
- [ ] Yellow mung beans (1.5 cups)
- [ ] Tomatoes, onion, garlic
- [ ] Ghee, turmeric, garam masala
- [ ] Ginger, bay leaf, chili
- [ ] Fresh coriander

## Steps
1. Cook mung beans until soft. Prepare tadka (ghee with spices)
2. Combine. Cook 10 min. Garnish with coriander
