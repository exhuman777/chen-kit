# Recipe: Japanese Dhal (Fusion)
tags: dinner, ayurveda
time: 40 min
source: PDF ayurvedic

## Ingredients
- [ ] Red lentils
- [ ] Miso paste, soy sauce
- [ ] Ginger, garlic
- [ ] Silken tofu
- [ ] Wakame or nori

## Steps
1. Cook lentils. Add miso (do not boil after adding)
2. Serve with tofu and seaweed
