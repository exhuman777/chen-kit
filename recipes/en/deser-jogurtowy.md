# Recipe: Blissful Yogurt Dessert
tags: breakfast, dessert
time: 10 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Oat yogurt
- [ ] Tahini
- [ ] Honey (or maple syrup)
- [ ] Nuts (almonds, pine nuts)
- [ ] Coconut oil, cinnamon
- [ ] Rose petal jam

## Steps
1. Toast nuts in oil with cinnamon and syrup
2. Mix part of the yogurt with the jam
3. Layer in a bowl
