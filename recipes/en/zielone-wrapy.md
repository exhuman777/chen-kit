# Recipe: Green Wraps (Ayurveda)
tags: lunch, ayurveda
time: 25 min
source: PDF ayurvedic

## Ingredients
- [ ] Tortilla (rice or wheat)
- [ ] Mung hummus or regular hummus
- [ ] Spinach, cucumber, avocado
- [ ] Sprouts, carrot
- [ ] Tahini-lemon sauce

## Steps
1. Spread hummus on tortilla. Arrange green vegetables
2. Drizzle with sauce. Roll up tightly. Cut in half
