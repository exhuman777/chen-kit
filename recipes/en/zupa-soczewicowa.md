# Recipe: Hearty Lentil Soup with Roasted Potatoes
tags: lunch, dinner, soup
time: 45 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 tablespoons olive oil
- [ ] Celery stalk
- [ ] Teaspoon of sweet paprika
- [ ] 2 garlic cloves
- [ ] 230 g green lentils (soaked overnight)
- [ ] Carrot
- [ ] Tablespoon of tomato paste
- [ ] Thyme (fresh or dried)
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon gochujang paste
- [ ] 1.25 L water + 2.5 bouillon cubes
- [ ] 3 potatoes
- [ ] Potato spices: paprika, garlic, salt
- [ ] 1 red onion (sliced into rings)
- [ ] Flour, starch, breadcrumbs (for onion)
- [ ] Jar of sun-dried tomatoes, capers (for sauce)

## Steps
1. Soup: Saute carrot and celery (8 min). Add garlic, spices, tomato paste. Add liquids and lentils. Cook 20-25 min
2. Potatoes: Roast slices with spices and olive oil (30 min, 200C)
3. Onion rings: Make batter from water, flour, and starch. Bread the rings, deep-fry
4. Sauce: Blend sun-dried tomatoes (with their oil), capers, and water
