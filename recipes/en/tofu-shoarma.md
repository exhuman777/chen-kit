# Recipe: Tofu Shawarma
tags: dinner
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Plain tofu (sliced)
- [ ] Shawarma seasoning (cumin, coriander, paprika, cinnamon)
- [ ] Onion, bell pepper
- [ ] Pita, garlic sauce

## Steps
1. Marinate tofu in spices. Roast or pan-fry
2. Saute onion and bell pepper. Serve in pita with sauce
