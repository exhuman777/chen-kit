# Recipe: Broccoli Soup (Ayurveda)
tags: soup, ayurveda
time: 30 min
source: PDF ayurvedic

## Ingredients
- [ ] Broccoli (large head)
- [ ] Onion, garlic, ginger
- [ ] Vegetable broth
- [ ] Ghee, turmeric, cumin
- [ ] Coconut milk

## Steps
1. Saute onion in ghee with spices. Add broccoli and broth
2. Cook 15 min. Blend. Add coconut milk
