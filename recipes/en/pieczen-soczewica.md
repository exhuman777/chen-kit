# Recipe: Lentil Loaf
tags: dinner
time: 60 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 cups cooked lentils
- [ ] 1 cup nuts
- [ ] Mushrooms, onion, garlic, carrot
- [ ] Oat flakes, breadcrumbs
- [ ] Soy sauce, tomato paste, flaxseed
- [ ] Glaze: ketchup, maple syrup

## Steps
1. Saute the vegetables
2. Mix with lentils and the rest
3. Bake in a loaf pan 45-50 min (180C)
