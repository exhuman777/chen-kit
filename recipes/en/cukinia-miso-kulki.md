# Recipe: Miso Zucchini (Rice Balls)
tags: lunch, snack
time: 20 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Sushi rice (cooked)
- [ ] Zucchini (grated)
- [ ] Miso, sesame oil, soy sauce
- [ ] Ginger, maple syrup

## Steps
1. Fry zucchini with spices. Add miso at the end
2. Shape rice balls with the filling
