# Recipe: Vegan Lemon Madeleines
tags: dessert
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 100g wheat flour + 3 tablespoons almond flour
- [ ] 55 g cane sugar
- [ ] Plant-based milk, coconut oil
- [ ] Maple syrup, vanilla
- [ ] Lemon (zest and juice)

## Steps
1. Mix dry and wet ingredients. Chill the batter for 30 min
2. Bake 18 min at 190C
