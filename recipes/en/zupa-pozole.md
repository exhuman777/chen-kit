# Recipe: Pozole Soup
tags: soup, mexican
time: 45 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Potatoes, cumin, oregano
- [ ] White beans, corn
- [ ] Corona beer (200ml), lime
- [ ] Kale
- [ ] Vegan schnitzel (fried)
- [ ] Radish, red cabbage, avocado (salad)

## Steps
1. Cook soup (potatoes, beans, beer)
2. Serve with schnitzel and salad
