# Recipe: Falafel-Style Patties with Tzatziki Sauce
tags: dinner, lunch
time: 40 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Jar of chickpeas
- [ ] Garlic, onion, fresh parsley
- [ ] 2 tablespoons wheat flour
- [ ] Cumin
- [ ] Cucumber, plant-based yogurt (tzatziki)

## Steps
1. Blend ingredients into patties. Shape and bake (30 min, 180C)
2. Make tzatziki (grated cucumber + yogurt + garlic)
