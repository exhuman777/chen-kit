# Recipe: Tofu in Marry Me Sauce
tags: dinner
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Smoked tofu
- [ ] Cornstarch (for coating)
- [ ] 250 g gnocchi (NOTE: starchy product)
- [ ] Sun-dried tomatoes, coconut milk, broth
- [ ] Nutritional yeast, spinach

## Steps
1. Fry coated tofu. Fry gnocchi
2. Make sauce from onion, tomatoes, milk, and broth
3. Combine everything with spinach
