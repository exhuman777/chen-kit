# Recipe: Aromatic Marinated Tofu
tags: protein, lunch
time: 30 min + overnight
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Plain tofu
- [ ] Miso
- [ ] Marinade: olive oil, maple syrup, tamari, garlic, shallots, mustard

## Steps
1. Marinate tofu overnight
2. Fry while basting with marinade. At the end, add miso dissolved in water
