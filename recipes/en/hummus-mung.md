# Recipe: Mung Hummus (Ayurveda)
tags: snack, ayurveda
time: 30 min
source: PDF ayurvedic

## Ingredients
- [ ] Mung beans (cooked)
- [ ] Tahini, lemon
- [ ] Garlic, cumin
- [ ] Olive oil, salt

## Steps
1. Blend mung beans with tahini, lemon and spices
2. Add olive oil to desired consistency. Serve with vegetables
