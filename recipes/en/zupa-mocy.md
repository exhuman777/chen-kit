# Recipe: Power Soup
tags: soup, health, tcm
time: 60 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Vegetables for broth (parsley root, carrot, leek, celery, cabbage)
- [ ] 2 red dates (Jujubes)
- [ ] 100g red lentils
- [ ] Turmeric, ginger, Goji berries
- [ ] Astragalus, Dang Gui
- [ ] Spinach, sesame oil

## Steps
1. Cook broth with dates
2. Saute ginger/garlic, add lentils, broth, and herbs
3. Cook 20 min. Add spinach
