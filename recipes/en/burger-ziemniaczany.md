# Recipe: Burger with Potato Patty
tags: dinner
time: 40 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Hamburger bun
- [ ] Pickle and onion sauce
- [ ] Beyond patty (300g)
- [ ] Potato patty (potatoes, flour)
- [ ] Nut and sesame sauce
- [ ] Vegan cheese

## Steps
1. Fry Beyond patties
2. Make potato patties and fry
3. Assemble burger with both types of sauce
