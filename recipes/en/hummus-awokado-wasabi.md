# Recipe: Avocado Wasabi Hummus
tags: snack, dip
time: 10 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Jar of chickpeas (drained)
- [ ] Flesh of 1 avocado
- [ ] Teaspoon of wasabi
- [ ] Juice of half a lime
- [ ] Tablespoon of vegan Worcestershire sauce
- [ ] Tablespoon of olive oil
- [ ] 50 ml water
- [ ] 2 tablespoons tahini
- [ ] Half a garlic clove
- [ ] Salt, pepper, sesame seeds, dill

## Steps
1. Blend all ingredients
2. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with sesame seeds and dill
