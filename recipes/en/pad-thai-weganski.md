# Recipe: Simple Vegan Pad Thai
tags: lunch, dinner
time: 25 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 150-200g rice noodles
- [ ] Olive oil, garlic, shallots
- [ ] Green bell pepper, carrot
- [ ] Tofu
- [ ] Curry seasoning
- [ ] Soy sauce, muscovado sugar (NOTE: avoid sugar)
- [ ] Lime
- [ ] Peanuts, bean sprouts, cilantro

## Steps
1. Saute the onion. Add tofu and curry (5 min)
2. Add vegetables and sauces (7 min)
3. Add cooked noodles and lime. Toss together
