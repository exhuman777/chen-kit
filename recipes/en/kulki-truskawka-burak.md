# Recipe: Strawberry-Beet Balls
tags: snack, dessert, raw
time: 15 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Freeze-dried strawberries
- [ ] Freeze-dried beet slices
- [ ] Sunflower seed butter
- [ ] Almond-coconut-date butter
- [ ] Coconut flakes

## Steps
1. Grind strawberries and beets
2. Add butters until you get a pliable dough
3. Shape into balls, roll in coconut flakes
