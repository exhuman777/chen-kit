# Recipe: Chocolate Nut Blondies
tags: dessert
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] One jar of chickpeas
- [ ] 4 tablespoons maple syrup
- [ ] 30 g rice flour
- [ ] 3 tablespoons peanut butter (NOTE: avoid per guidelines)
- [ ] 2 tablespoons ground hazelnuts
- [ ] Half a bar of dark chocolate
- [ ] Vanilla extract
- [ ] 4 tablespoons maple sugar
- [ ] Baking powder

## Steps
1. Blend all ingredients (except chocolate)
2. Pour into a baking tin, top with chocolate
3. Bake 25 min (180C). Serve with pudding
