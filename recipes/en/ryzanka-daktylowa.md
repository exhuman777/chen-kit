# Recipe: Rice Flake Porridge with Dates and Cinnamon
tags: breakfast, sweet
time: 10 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 6 tablespoons rice flakes
- [ ] 2 dates
- [ ] Cinnamon
- [ ] Plant-based milk
- [ ] Beet molasses

## Steps
1. Cook everything (except molasses) for 5 min
2. Serve with molasses
