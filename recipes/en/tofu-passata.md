# Recipe: Tofu in Tomato Passata
tags: dinner
time: 25 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Smoked or plain tofu
- [ ] Tomato passata
- [ ] Onion, garlic, basil
- [ ] Olive oil, salt, pepper

## Steps
1. Fry tofu until golden. Set aside
2. Saute onion and garlic. Add passata. Add tofu. Simmer 10 min
