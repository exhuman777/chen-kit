# Recipe: Universal Dinner Bowl
tags: dinner
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Base: rice, quinoa, or groats
- [ ] Protein: tofu, tempeh, or plant-based alternative
- [ ] Vegetables: roasted or raw
- [ ] Sauce: tahini, peanut, or Asian-style

## Steps
1. Prepare the base. Cook the protein. Prepare the vegetables
2. Arrange in a bowl. Drizzle with sauce. Sprinkle with seeds
