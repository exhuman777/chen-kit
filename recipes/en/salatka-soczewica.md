# Recipe: Lentil Salad
tags: lunch, dinner
time: 40 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 1 cup lentils (green/brown)
- [ ] Cucumber, cherry tomatoes
- [ ] Red onion, parsley
- [ ] Feta (optional - NOTE: avoid cheese per guidelines)
- [ ] Dressing: olive oil, lemon, garlic

## Steps
1. Cook lentils
2. Chop vegetables. Mix with dressing
