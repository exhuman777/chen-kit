# Recipe: No-Bake Nut and Cinnamon Rolls
tags: dessert
time: 20 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Rolled oats
- [ ] Dates
- [ ] Nut butter (NOTE: avoid per guidelines)
- [ ] Cinnamon, cardamom, cocoa

## Steps
1. Make a cream from oatmeal and nut butter
2. Make dough from oats and dates
3. Spread, roll up, refrigerate
