# Recipe: Warm Lentil, Sweet Potato, and Arugula Salad
tags: lunch, dinner
time: 40 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Sweet potato
- [ ] 100 g brown lentils
- [ ] Arugula
- [ ] Onion, garlic
- [ ] Walnuts, pumpkin seeds
- [ ] Dressing: olive oil, lime, mustard

## Steps
1. Roast sweet potato. Cook lentils
2. Saute onion with lentils
3. Mix with arugula and dressing
