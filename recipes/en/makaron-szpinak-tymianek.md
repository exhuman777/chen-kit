# Recipe: Creamy Vegan Pasta with Spinach and Thyme
tags: lunch, dinner
time: 25 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Fusilli pasta
- [ ] Cashews (soaked)
- [ ] Soy milk, nutritional yeast
- [ ] Thyme, fennel, spinach

## Steps
1. Blend the cashews into a sauce
2. Saute the onion and herbs
3. Add the sauce and spinach. Toss with the pasta
