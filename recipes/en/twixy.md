# Recipe: Vegan Twix Bars
tags: dessert
time: 60 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Flour, coconut oil (base)
- [ ] Dates, nut butter (caramel)
- [ ] Vegan chocolate (topping)

## Steps
1. Make shortcrust pastry. Bake 15 min
2. Prepare date caramel. Spread on the base
3. Cover with chocolate. Refrigerate. Cut into bars
