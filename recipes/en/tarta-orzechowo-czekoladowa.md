# Recipe: Vegan Nut and Chocolate Tart
tags: dessert
time: 20 min + chilling
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 150g nuts (hazelnuts, walnuts, almonds)
- [ ] 50g shredded coconut
- [ ] 70g nut butter, date syrup (Base)
- [ ] Coconut milk (solid part), 100g dark chocolate (Cream)

## Steps
1. Blend base ingredients, press into a tart pan
2. Melt chocolate in coconut milk. Pour over the base. Chill 3h
