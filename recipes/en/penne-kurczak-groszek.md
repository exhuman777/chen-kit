# Recipe: Penne with Vegan Chicken and Green Peas
tags: lunch, dinner
time: 20 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Plantway chicken
- [ ] Jar of green peas
- [ ] 250 g penne
- [ ] 200 ml coconut milk
- [ ] Vegan cheese, nutritional yeast
- [ ] Cumin, lime

## Steps
1. Saute the chicken with cumin. Add peas
2. Add pasta and sauce (coconut milk + cheese)
