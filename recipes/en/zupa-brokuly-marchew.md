# Recipe: Clear Broccoli and Carrot Soup
tags: soup, lunch, pdf
time: 15 min
source: Simple Ayurvedic Recipes PDF

## Ingredients
- [ ] 3 carrots
- [ ] 2 cups broccoli
- [ ] Ghee
- [ ] Cumin, turmeric
- [ ] Water, fresh cilantro

## Steps
1. Toast spices in ghee
2. Add vegetables, cover with water. Cook until tender
3. Add cilantro at the end
