# Recipe: Savory Curry Cake with Carrot and Zucchini
tags: lunch, snack
time: 50 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 140 g chickpea flour
- [ ] 300 ml broth (200 for cake, 100 for sauce)
- [ ] 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast flakes
- [ ] Thyme, curry, salt, pepper
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- [ ] 2 teaspoons chia seeds + water
- [ ] 35 g pumpkin seeds
- [ ] White onion
- [ ] Fresh parsley
- [ ] Carrot (grated)
- [ ] 1/2 zucchini (grated)
- [ ] Potato starch (1 teaspoon)
- [ ] 300 g coconut yogurt
- [ ] Tablespoon of ketchup

## Steps
1. Cake: Saute most of the onion. Mix flour, yeast flakes, baking powder, spices. Add 200ml broth, chia, olive oil. Add vegetables and seeds. Bake 35-40 min (180C)
2. Sauce: Saute remaining onion with curry. Add starch, 100ml broth. Bring to a boil. Add yogurt and ketchup
