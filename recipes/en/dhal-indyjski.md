# Recipe: Classic Indian Dhal
tags: dinner, ayurveda
time: 50 min
source: PDF ayurvedic

## Ingredients
- [ ] Red lentils (2 cups)
- [ ] Onion, garlic, ginger
- [ ] Tomatoes, spinach
- [ ] Ghee, turmeric, cumin, coriander
- [ ] Garam masala, chili

## Steps
1. Cook lentils with turmeric. Prepare tadka
2. Add tomatoes and spinach. Cook 15 min. Sprinkle with garam masala
