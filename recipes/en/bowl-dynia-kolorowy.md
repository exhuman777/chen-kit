# Recipe: Colorful Pumpkin Bowl
tags: dinner
time: 40 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Roasted pumpkin
- [ ] Red cabbage (sauerkraut or fresh)
- [ ] Chickpeas, spinach
- [ ] Tahini, lemon

## Steps
1. Roast pumpkin 30 min. Prepare remaining ingredients
2. Arrange colorfully in a bowl. Drizzle with tahini sauce
