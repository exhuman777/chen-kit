# Recipe: Chocolate Orange Cookies
tags: dessert
time: 25 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Wheat flour (80g), oat flour (25g)
- [ ] Flaxseed (soaked)
- [ ] Cocoa, brown sugar
- [ ] Oil, orange extract, orange zest
- [ ] Vegan chocolate (chunks)

## Steps
1. Mix ingredients. Shape into balls. Bake 14 min at 180C
