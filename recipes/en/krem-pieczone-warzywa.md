# Recipe: Roasted Vegetable Cream Soup
tags: soup, dinner
time: 50 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Pumpkin, carrot, onion, garlic
- [ ] Olive oil, thyme, rosemary
- [ ] Vegetable broth
- [ ] Coconut cream

## Steps
1. Roast vegetables 30 min at 200C. Blend with broth
2. Add cream. Serve with croutons
