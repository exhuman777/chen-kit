# Recipe: Egg-Free Huevos Rancheros
tags: breakfast, lunch
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 tortillas
- [ ] Black beans (fried with milk and cumin)
- [ ] Tofu (scramble)
- [ ] Ranchero sauce: Passata, jalapeno, cumin, oregano
- [ ] Avocado, cilantro, lime

## Steps
1. Cook ranchero sauce
2. Warm beans (mash them). Make tofu scramble
3. Layer on tortillas: beans, tofu, sauce and toppings
