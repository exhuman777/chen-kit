# Recipe: Vegan French Toast
tags: breakfast, sweet
time: 20 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Plant-based milk, spelt flour
- [ ] Maple syrup, vanilla, cinnamon
- [ ] Bread (4-6 slices)
- [ ] Pecans, dates (toppings)

## Steps
1. Make batter from milk, flour, and spices
2. Dip bread, fry in vegan butter until golden
3. Serve with toasted nuts and syrup
