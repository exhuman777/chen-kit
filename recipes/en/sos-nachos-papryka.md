# Recipe: Nacho Dip with Bell Pepper and Pumpkin Seeds
tags: snack, dip
time: 20 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
- [ ] 3 bell peppers (assorted colors)
- [ ] Garlic, cumin, smoked paprika
- [ ] Lime juice, apple cider vinegar
- [ ] Olive oil, agave syrup

## Steps
1. Saute bell peppers until soft, peel the skin
2. Saute garlic and seeds with cumin
3. Blend everything with the remaining ingredients
