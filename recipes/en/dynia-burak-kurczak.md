# Recipe: Roasted Pumpkin with Beet and Chicken
tags: dinner, lunch, bowl
time: 50 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 1 hokkaido pumpkin
- [ ] 2 small beets
- [ ] Rosemary, cumin, curry, salt
- [ ] Olive oil, water
- [ ] Plant-based chicken (e.g. Plantway)
- [ ] Avocado, onion, tomato, lime (Guacamole)
- [ ] Jar of artichokes in oil

## Steps
1. Roast pumpkin and beets with spices and water (45 min, 180C)
2. Pan-fry chicken. Make guacamole
3. Arrange in a bowl: roasted vegetables, chicken, guacamole, artichokes
