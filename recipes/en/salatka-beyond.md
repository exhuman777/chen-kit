# Recipe: Quick Salad with Beyond Meat
tags: lunch, dinner, salad
time: 20 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Head of romaine lettuce
- [ ] Yellow bell pepper
- [ ] 1 large date (pitted)
- [ ] Beyond Meat / Tofu / Mushrooms (pan-fried)
- [ ] Nut butter (NOTE: avoid)
- [ ] Tahini
- [ ] Oat-almond milk
- [ ] Golden flaxseed
- [ ] Black sesame

## Steps
1. Tear lettuce, chop bell pepper and date
2. Add pan-fried protein
3. Make dressing from nut butter, tahini, and milk
4. Sprinkle with ground flaxseed and sesame
