# Recipe: Mac n Cheese Burger in a Donut Bun
tags: dinner, cheat-meal
time: 40 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Homemade donut
- [ ] 2 tablespoons vegan mayo
- [ ] 2 teaspoons Sarepska mustard
- [ ] 2 half-sour pickles
- [ ] 1/4 white onion
- [ ] Juice of 1/4 lime
- [ ] Plantway burger patty
- [ ] 150 g gomitini pasta
- [ ] 50 g vegan cheese
- [ ] 100 ml almond milk
- [ ] Teaspoon of turmeric
- [ ] Pepper

## Steps
1. Prepare the pickle-onion sauce (mayo, mustard, pickles, onion, lime)
2. Fry the burger patty
3. Prepare Mac n Cheese: heat pasta, cheese, milk, turmeric, and pepper
4. Assemble: Donut, sauce, burger, sauce, mac n cheese, glazed donut top

## Notes
WARNING: This recipe breaks the "Sugar + Flour + Fat" rule. Treat as an exception.
