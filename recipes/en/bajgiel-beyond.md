# Recipe: Bagel with Beyond Meat
tags: breakfast, lunch, sandwich
time: 20 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Poppy seed bagel (NOTE: contains wheat flour)
- [ ] Vegan butter
- [ ] Peanut butter, tahini, hazelnut milk (for sauce)
- [ ] Romaine lettuce, fresh cucumber
- [ ] Beyond Meat
- [ ] Red onion, curry, pepper
- [ ] Vegan cheese

## Steps
1. Toast bagel halves in butter
2. Fry Beyond Meat with onion and spices. Melt cheese on top
3. Layer with nut butter sauce and vegetables
