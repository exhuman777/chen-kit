# Recipe: Tofu Scrambled Eggs with Kala Namak
tags: breakfast
time: 15 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Plain tofu
- [ ] Onion, vegan butter
- [ ] Curry, kala namak salt (2-4 pinches)
- [ ] Oat milk, nutritional yeast
- [ ] Chives

## Steps
1. Saute onion with curry and kala namak
2. Add tofu. Pour in milk and nutritional yeast. Fry 5 min
