# Recipe: Vegan Mushroom Tamales
tags: dinner, complex
time: 90 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 90 g cornmeal
- [ ] Warm broth
- [ ] Vegan butter, baking powder
- [ ] 4 mushrooms, onion, garlic
- [ ] Smoked paprika, cumin, soy sauce
- [ ] Parchment paper

## Steps
1. Make dough from cornmeal, broth, and butter
2. Make filling from mushrooms and onion (fry with spices)
3. Form parcels in parchment paper. Steam for 60 min
