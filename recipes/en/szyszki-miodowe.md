# Recipe: Honey Pinecones (vegan honey)
tags: dessert, snack
time: 15 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Cornflakes
- [ ] Vegan honey or maple syrup
- [ ] Nut butter
- [ ] Chocolate (for dipping)

## Steps
1. Mix flakes with honey and nut butter. Shape into pinecones
2. Dip in chocolate. Refrigerate
