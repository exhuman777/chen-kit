# Recipe: Chapati with Tofu
tags: dinner, lunch
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 4 tablespoons spelt flour (NOTE: avoid flour per guidelines)
- [ ] Olive oil, salt, water (for chapati)
- [ ] Block of smoked tofu (crumbled)
- [ ] Onion, soy sauce, maple syrup
- [ ] Smoked paprika, coriander, garam masala, cumin

## Steps
1. Chapati: Knead dough, fry flatbreads in a pan
2. Tofu: Saute onion with spices. Add sauces and tofu. Fry 7-10 min
