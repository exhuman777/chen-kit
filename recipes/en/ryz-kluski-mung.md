# Recipe: Rice and Mung Bean Stew (Ayurveda)
tags: dinner, ayurveda
time: 40 min
source: PDF ayurvedic

## Ingredients
- [ ] Basmati rice (1 cup)
- [ ] Mung beans (1/2 cup)
- [ ] Ghee or coconut oil
- [ ] Turmeric, cumin, coriander
- [ ] Fresh ginger, bay leaf

## Steps
1. Soak mung beans for 2h. Cook with rice in a 3:1 water ratio
2. Add ghee with spices. Cook until tender
