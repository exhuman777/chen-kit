# Recipe: Breakfast Tofu Curry
tags: breakfast
time: 20 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Plain tofu (crumbled)
- [ ] Onion, bell pepper, tomato
- [ ] Curry, turmeric, cumin
- [ ] Spinach, cilantro

## Steps
1. Saute onion. Add tofu and spices
2. Add vegetables. Simmer 10 min. Garnish with cilantro
