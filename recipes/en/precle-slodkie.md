# Recipe: Sweet and Salty Pretzels
tags: snack
time: 90 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Spelt flour (NOTE: avoid flour per guidelines)
- [ ] Yeast, coconut sugar
- [ ] Vegan butter, oat milk
- [ ] Cinnamon, salt

## Steps
1. Knead the dough, let it rise for 1h
2. Shape the pretzels, blanch in water with sugar
3. Bake 13 min (205C). Drizzle with butter
