# Recipe: Vegan Bangers and Mash (Kofta)
tags: dinner, complex
time: 90 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Wheat gluten (Koftas - prepared the day before)
- [ ] Potatoes, milk, butter, nutritional yeast flakes (Mash)
- [ ] Gravy, peas, carrots

## Steps
1. Koftas steamed and baked the day before, grill them
2. Serve with mash and gravy
