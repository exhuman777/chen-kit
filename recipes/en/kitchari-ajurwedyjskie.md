# Recipe: Ayurvedic Kitchari with Vegetables
tags: lunch, dinner, ayurveda
time: 45 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 90 g basmati/jasmine rice (soaked)
- [ ] 50 g mung beans (soaked)
- [ ] Carrot, spinach, shallot, zucchini
- [ ] Ghee, cumin, mustard seeds, turmeric, ginger, coriander, cardamom

## Steps
1. Saute spices and shallot in ghee
2. Add rice, mung beans, water. Cook 25 min
3. Add hard vegetables (15 min), then spinach (5 min)
