# Recipe: Vegan Upside-Down Apple and Pear Cake
tags: dessert, cake
time: 60 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 150 g wheat flour
- [ ] Brown sugar (for cake and fruit)
- [ ] Vegan margarine (cold + for fruit)
- [ ] Cold water
- [ ] 3 apples, 2 pears

## Steps
1. Dough: Knead flour, sugar, margarine, water. Chill
2. Fruit: Melt sugar in a pan, add margarine and fruit. Cook 15 min
3. Arrange fruit in a baking tin, cover with rolled-out dough
4. Bake 35 min at 190C. Flip after cooling

## Notes
Contains combination of sugar + flour + fat (avoid/treat as exception)
