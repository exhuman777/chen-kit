# Recipe: Mashed Potatoes with Mushroom-White Bean Sauce
tags: lunch, dinner
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 tablespoons olive oil
- [ ] Red onion, garlic
- [ ] Mushrooms
- [ ] Jar of white beans
- [ ] Celery stalks
- [ ] Thyme, herbes de Provence
- [ ] 500 ml broth
- [ ] 3 tablespoons rice flour
- [ ] 60 ml coconut milk
- [ ] Potatoes, vegan butter, milk (for the mash)

## Steps
1. Sauce: Saute onion and mushrooms (10 min). Add celery and garlic. Add herbs, broth, beans, seasonings, coconut milk, and flour. Cook until thickened
2. Mash: Boil potatoes, mash with butter and milk
