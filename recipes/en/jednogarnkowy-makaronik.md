# Recipe: One-Pot Pasta
tags: dinner
time: 25 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Pasta (any short variety)
- [ ] Onion, garlic, tomatoes
- [ ] Vegetable broth
- [ ] Spinach, nutritional yeast

## Steps
1. Saute onion and garlic. Add tomatoes and broth
2. Add pasta, cook until tender. Add spinach and nutritional yeast
