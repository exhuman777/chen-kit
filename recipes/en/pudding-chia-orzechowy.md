# Recipe: Nutty Chia Pudding with Cream
tags: breakfast, dessert
time: 15 min + chilling
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 3 tablespoons chia seeds
- [ ] 2 tablespoons tahini
- [ ] 2 tablespoons hazelnuts
- [ ] Cup of water
- [ ] Turmeric, salt
- [ ] 3 dates (for the cream)
- [ ] 1/3 hazelnut milk
- [ ] Nut butter (for the cream - NOTE: avoid per guidelines)

## Steps
1. Pudding: Blend chia, tahini, nuts, water, turmeric. Refrigerate for 3-4h (stir every hour)
2. Cream: Blend dates, milk, nut butter, vanilla
3. Serve pudding with cream and toppings (strawberries, cocoa)
