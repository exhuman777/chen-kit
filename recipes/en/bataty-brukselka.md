# Recipe: Nutty Spiced Sweet Potatoes with Brussels Sprouts
tags: dinner, lunch, vegan
time: 70 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 1 sweet potato (large)
- [ ] 5 tablespoons sunflower oil
- [ ] Five-spice powder
- [ ] 3 tablespoons hazelnut butter (NOTE: avoid)
- [ ] 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- [ ] Brussels sprouts
- [ ] 2 tablespoons olive oil
- [ ] 1 cm piece of old ginger
- [ ] 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- [ ] 1 grated carrot
- [ ] Half a garlic clove
- [ ] 3 tablespoons cornflakes
- [ ] 2 tablespoons tahini (for sauce)
- [ ] Oat milk (for sauce)

## Steps
1. Sweet potatoes: Score, brush with oil, salt. Bake 1h at 200-220C
2. Glaze: Toast five-spice powder in oil, add nut butter and syrup. Pour over sweet potatoes, bake 10 min at 190C
3. Brussels sprouts: Saute ginger and sprouts in olive oil with water. Add soy sauce and carrot (5 min). Add syrup, garlic, cornflakes
4. Sauce: Mix nut butter, tahini, and oat milk
