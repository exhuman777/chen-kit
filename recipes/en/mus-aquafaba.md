# Recipe: Aquafaba Chocolate Mousse
tags: dessert
time: 20 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Aquafaba (chickpea brine)
- [ ] Dark chocolate (melted)
- [ ] Powdered sugar, vanilla

## Steps
1. Whip the aquafaba to stiff peaks. Let the chocolate cool
2. Gently fold the chocolate into the foam. Chill for 2h
