# Recipe: Puffed Chocolate Bars
tags: dessert, snack
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Puffed rice, oat flakes, cocoa
- [ ] Nut butter, maple syrup
- [ ] Vegan chocolate (topping)

## Steps
1. Mix dry ingredients. Combine with butter and syrup
2. Shape into bars. Drizzle with chocolate. Chill
