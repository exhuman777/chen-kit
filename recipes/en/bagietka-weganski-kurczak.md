# Recipe: Baguette with Vegan Chicken
tags: lunch, dinner
time: 15 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Baguette
- [ ] Iceberg lettuce
- [ ] Vegan mayo
- [ ] Green tabasco
- [ ] Pickled gherkins
- [ ] Onion, dill, chives
- [ ] Plant-based chicken gyros (e.g. Plantway)
- [ ] Teaspoon of curry
- [ ] Vegan cheese slices

## Steps
1. Warm the baguette
2. Make sauce from mayo, tabasco, gherkins, and herbs
3. Fry chicken with onion and curry
4. Assemble sandwich: lettuce, sauce, chicken, cheese
