# Recipe: Miso Tofu with Soba Noodles
tags: lunch, dinner
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 180 g tofu
- [ ] Cornmeal breading
- [ ] Onion, garlic, ginger
- [ ] Miso paste, soy sauce, maple syrup
- [ ] Soba noodles

## Steps
1. Fry crumbled tofu with spices
2. Add miso sauce. Serve with noodles
