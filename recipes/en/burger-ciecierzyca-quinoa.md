# Recipe: Chickpea and Quinoa Burger
tags: lunch, dinner
time: 40 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Half a jar of chickpeas
- [ ] 30 g cooked quinoa
- [ ] Onion, garlic
- [ ] Flaxseed (as egg substitute)
- [ ] Oat flour (3 tablespoons)
- [ ] Cumin, smoked paprika

## Steps
1. Make a "flax egg"
2. Mix mashed chickpeas with the rest
3. Shape into patties, bake 25 min (190C)
