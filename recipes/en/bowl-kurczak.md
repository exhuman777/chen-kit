# Recipe: Bowl with Chicken (or Tofu)
tags: dinner, lunch
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Chicken or tofu
- [ ] Brown rice or quinoa
- [ ] Avocado, cucumber, carrot
- [ ] Edamame, sesame
- [ ] Soy sauce, sesame oil

## Steps
1. Fry chicken/tofu. Cook rice
2. Arrange in a bowl with vegetables. Drizzle with sauce
