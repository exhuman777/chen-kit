# Recipe: Spaghetti with Pumpkin Seed Pesto
tags: lunch, dinner
time: 25 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 50g pumpkin seeds (toasted)
- [ ] 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
- [ ] Garlic, lemon, olive oil, parsley, arugula
- [ ] 250 g pasta

## Steps
1. Blend ingredients into pesto
2. Cook pasta (reserve pasta water)
3. Toss pasta with pesto and cooking water
