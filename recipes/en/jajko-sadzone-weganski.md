# Recipe: Sunny Vegan Breakfast (Fried Egg)
tags: breakfast
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Sauce: Tomato, onion, garlic, cumin, oregano
- [ ] Beans: Red kidney beans, plant milk, smoked paprika
- [ ] White: Milk, spelt flour, kala namak salt, starch
- [ ] Yolk: Carrot/pumpkin, milk, nutritional yeast, vinegar

## Steps
1. Make tomato sauce
2. Pan-fry beans with spices
3. Pan-fry the "white" from batter, place the "yolk" puree in the center
