# Recipe: Coconut Oatmeal with Nut Butter
tags: breakfast, vegan, sweet
time: 15 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 1/2 cup oat flakes (rinsed)
- [ ] 1 cup coconut milk
- [ ] 1 tablespoon nut butter (NOTE: avoid per guidelines)
- [ ] 1 tablespoon maple syrup
- [ ] Pinch of Himalayan salt
- [ ] 2 tablespoons coconut flakes
- [ ] A few chopped pistachios

## Steps
1. In a saucepan, heat the coconut milk with oat flakes, coconut flakes, and a pinch of salt
2. Cook on low heat for about 10 minutes
3. Remove from heat
4. Transfer to a bowl and add nut butter, pistachios, and maple syrup

## Notes
Per guidelines: avoid nut butter (can be omitted).
