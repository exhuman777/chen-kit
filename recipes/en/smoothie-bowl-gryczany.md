# Recipe: Oriental Buckwheat Smoothie Bowl
tags: breakfast, sweet
time: 20 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 50g roasted buckwheat groats (or unroasted)
- [ ] Tablespoon of sunflower oil
- [ ] Star anise
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon cumin
- [ ] Tablespoon of maple syrup (for nuts) + to taste
- [ ] 3 dates
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon Ceylon cinnamon
- [ ] Handful of walnuts and blanched almonds
- [ ] 2 tablespoons tahini
- [ ] Tablespoon of coconut (chunks or shredded)
- [ ] 150-200 ml coconut milk
- [ ] 1 espresso
- [ ] Coconut yogurt
- [ ] Gomasio (black sesame with salt)

## Steps
1. Caramelized nuts: Toast fennel, anise, cumin, and cinnamon in oil. Add nuts, fry 2 min. Add maple syrup, fry 3-5 min
2. Smoothie: Blend cooked buckwheat, dates, tahini, coconut, milk, and coffee
3. Pour into bowls, top with yogurt, nuts, and gomasio
