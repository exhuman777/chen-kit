# Recipe: Holiday Roasted Pumpkin
tags: dinner, holiday
time: 50 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Hokkaido pumpkin
- [ ] Walnuts, cranberries
- [ ] Cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger
- [ ] Maple syrup, olive oil

## Steps
1. Cut the pumpkin. Drizzle with olive oil and syrup. Sprinkle with spices
2. Roast 40 min at 200C. Top with walnuts and cranberries
