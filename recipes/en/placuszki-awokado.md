# Recipe: Avocado Fritters
tags: breakfast, lunch
time: 20 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Avocado (mashed)
- [ ] Tablespoon of flaxseed + boiling water
- [ ] 3 tablespoons coconut flour
- [ ] Baking powder, salt, pepper
- [ ] Soy yogurt, herbs, lemon (for the sauce)

## Steps
1. Mix avocado, soaked flaxseed, flour, and seasonings
2. Fry small fritters in olive oil until golden
3. Serve with sauce (yogurt mixed with herbs and lemon)
