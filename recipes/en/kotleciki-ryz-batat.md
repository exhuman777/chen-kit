# Recipe: Rice and Sweet Potato Patties
tags: dinner
time: 40 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Cooked rice
- [ ] Baked sweet potato (mashed)
- [ ] Oat flakes, spices
- [ ] Oil for frying

## Steps
1. Mix rice with sweet potato and oat flakes. Add spices
2. Shape into patties. Fry until golden on each side
