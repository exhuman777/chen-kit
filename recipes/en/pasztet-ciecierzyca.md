# Recipe: Classic Chickpea Loaf/Pate
tags: dinner
time: 60 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 jars of chickpeas
- [ ] Onion, garlic, carrot, bell pepper
- [ ] Oat flakes, breadcrumbs
- [ ] Soy sauce, tomato paste
- [ ] Glaze: ketchup, syrup

## Steps
1. Saute the vegetables
2. Mash the chickpeas, mix with the rest
3. Bake 45 min (180C)
