# Recipe: Digestive Fire Boosting Breakfast Rice
tags: breakfast, ayurveda
time: 25 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 100g jasmine/basmati rice
- [ ] 50 ml coconut milk
- [ ] 400 ml water
- [ ] Turmeric, cinnamon, ginger, pepper

## Steps
1. Heat olive oil with spices
2. Add rice, water, and milk. Cook for 15 min
