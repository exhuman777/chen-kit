# Recipe: Shepherd's Pie (vegan)
tags: dinner
time: 60 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Potatoes (mashed)
- [ ] Green or brown lentils
- [ ] Carrot, peas, onion
- [ ] Broth, soy sauce, herbs

## Steps
1. Cook lentils with vegetables and seasonings
2. Transfer to a baking dish, top with mashed potatoes. Bake 30 min at 200C
