# Recipe: Pistachio Tortilla Lunch Wrap
tags: lunch
time: 20 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 tortillas
- [ ] Chickpeas, shallot, oregano, gochugaru (pan-fried)
- [ ] Pistachios (soaked), milk, avocado, dill (cream)
- [ ] Lime

## Steps
1. Saute chickpeas with spices
2. Blend pistachio-avocado cream
3. Assemble tortillas
