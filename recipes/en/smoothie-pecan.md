# Recipe: Smoothie with Caramelized Pecans
tags: breakfast, sweet
time: 15 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Coconut oil
- [ ] 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- [ ] 10 pecans
- [ ] Cinnamon
- [ ] 2 cups almond milk
- [ ] 3 dates

## Steps
1. Toast pecans with syrup and cinnamon in oil (5 min)
2. Blend with milk and dates
