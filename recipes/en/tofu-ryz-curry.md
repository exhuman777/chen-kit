# Recipe: My Signature Tofu with Curry Rice
tags: lunch, dinner
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Risotto rice
- [ ] Curry, cumin, vegan butter, sesame
- [ ] Smoked tofu (spices: oregano, paprika, nutritional yeast, nigella seeds)
- [ ] Salad: lettuce, cucumber, vinaigrette dressing

## Steps
1. Cook rice, mix with butter and curry
2. Fry tofu in stages, adding spices one by one every few minutes
3. Serve with salad
