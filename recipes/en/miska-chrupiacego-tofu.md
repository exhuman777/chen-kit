# Recipe: Crispy Tofu Bowl
tags: lunch, dinner
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] Smoked tofu
- [ ] Soy sauce, olive oil, maple syrup, garlic, ginger (marinade)
- [ ] Cornstarch
- [ ] Sriracha, sesame oil (sauce)
- [ ] Rice, kimchi, vegetables

## Steps
1. Marinate the tofu (15 min). Coat in cornstarch
2. Fry until crispy (5-7 min)
3. Make the sauce from sriracha, soy sauce, syrup, and oil
4. Serve over rice with vegetables
