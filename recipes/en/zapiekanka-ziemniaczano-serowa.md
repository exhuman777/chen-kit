# Recipe: Potato and Cheese Casserole with Spinach
tags: dinner, casserole
time: 60 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 1 kg potatoes (boiled)
- [ ] Cashew sauce (cashews, broth, nutritional yeast, garlic)
- [ ] Tofu, carrot, parsley (pan-fried)
- [ ] Spinach, vegan cheese

## Steps
1. Make sauce. Saute vegetables and tofu
2. Mix everything in a baking dish. Bake 40 min at 180C
