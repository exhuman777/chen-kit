# Recipe: Simple Sweet Potato Soup with Tofu
tags: soup, dinner
time: 30 min
source: exhto

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 sweet potatoes
- [ ] Broth, almond milk
- [ ] Smoked tofu
- [ ] Onion, garlic
- [ ] Spices (oregano, smoked paprika)

## Steps
1. Cook sweet potato soup
2. Fry tofu cubes with spices
3. Serve soup topped with tofu
