# Kitchari Basics
category: diet/ayurveda

## Rules
- [ ] Base: basmati rice + mung beans (split, yellow)
- [ ] Ratio: 1:1 or 2:1 (rice:mung)
- [ ] Tadka: Ghee + spices (cumin, turmeric, ginger, mustard)
- [ ] Seasonal vegetables: carrot, zucchini, spinach
- [ ] Cook until very soft consistency
- [ ] Fresh cilantro on top

## Notes
Kitchari is "medicine" in Ayurveda - easily digestible, cleansing, complete protein. Ideal during detox or illness.
