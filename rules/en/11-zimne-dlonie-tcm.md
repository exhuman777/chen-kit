# Cold Hands and Feet
category: health/tcm

## Rules
- [ ] Symptoms: cold extremities, fatigue, paleness, swelling
- [ ] Avoid cold drinks and raw foods
- [ ] Prefer cooked, warm meals
- [ ] Warming spices: ginger, cinnamon, pepper
- [ ] Foot soaking with ginger
- [ ] Daily physical exercise

## Notes
If symptoms are frequent - consult a TCM practitioner. It may be Qi stagnation or Yang deficiency.
