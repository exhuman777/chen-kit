## Protein Sources (Plant-Based)
- [ ] **Mung beans:** Detoxifies heavy metals, easily digestible (base for Kitchari).
- [ ] **Black lentils:** Strengthens kidneys (soak overnight).
- [ ] **Red lentils:** Quick, for Indian dishes (soak 1h).
- [ ] **Tempeh/Tofu:** Smoked, plain.
- [ ] **Nuts:** Walnuts ("brain food"), Almonds (without skin, soaked).
- [ ] **Golden flaxseed:** Ground or as a gel (for intestines).
- [ ] **Spirulina/Chlorella:** For anemia.

## Power Broth (Soup Enhancement)
To turn soup into medicine, add:
- [ ] Homemade, long-simmered broth.
- [ ] Fresh ginger (a piece).
- [ ] Goji berries (a teaspoon).
- [ ] Red dates (Jujubes) - 2 pieces, pitted (add at the beginning).
- [ ] Black sesame.

## Anti-Aging Herbs (Teas)
- [ ] **Rooibos + Hibiscus:** Antioxidants.
- [ ] **Green tea + Mint + Lemon:** Detox, collagen (natural glow).
- [ ] **Nettle + Horsetail:** Hair, nails.
- [ ] **Chamomile + Lavender:** Stress reduction (fewer wrinkles).
- [ ] **Ashwagandha + Rose:** Adaptogens for the skin.

## Notes
Remember to soak legumes (Beans: 10-12h, Lentils: min. 1h or overnight) for better digestion.
