# Nervous System Reset
category: health/wellness

## Rules
- [ ] 4-7-8 Breathing: Inhale (4s), Hold (7s), Exhale (8s) - repeat 3-5x
- [ ] Hand on heart and belly while breathing
- [ ] Stretching and "shaking out" tension from the body
- [ ] Walking barefoot (grounding)
- [ ] Temperature reset: cold water on face or alternate shower
- [ ] Warm bath with magnesium salt
- [ ] 2 minutes of silence or nature sounds (birds)
- [ ] Journal: ask "What do I need right now?"
- [ ] Self-compassion: "I don't have to rush"

## Notes
Apply during moments of anxiety, mental chaos, or body tension.
