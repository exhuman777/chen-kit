# Dates - Natural Sweetener
category: diet/ingredients

## Rules
- [ ] Fresh dates: for eating, smoothies, creams
- [ ] Soak 15-30 min before blending
- [ ] Date syrup: alternative to honey
- [ ] Red dates (Jujubes): for TCM healing soups
- [ ] Date cream: dates + milk + cacao
- [ ] For baking: replace sugar
- [ ] In energy balls: binding base

## Restrictions
- [ ] Do not combine with large amounts of fat and flour (unholy trinity!)

## Notes
Dates are one of the few permitted sweet additions. Natural, whole-food quality.
