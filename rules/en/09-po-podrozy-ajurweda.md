# Post-Travel Balance
category: health/ayurveda

## Rules
- [ ] Wake up early
- [ ] Oral cleansing (oil pulling)
- [ ] Warm water on an empty stomach
- [ ] Meditation and Nadhi Shodhana (alternate nostril) breathing
- [ ] Ginger tea or a ginger slice with rock salt 15 min before a meal
- [ ] Fennel tea
- [ ] Abdominal massage: circular motions clockwise
- [ ] Omega fatty acids: olive oil, flaxseed oil

## Notes
Apply after long journeys, jet lag, or disruption of circadian rhythm.
