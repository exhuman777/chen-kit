# Healthy Substitutes
category: diet/substitutes

## Rules
- [ ] Peanut butter -> Tahini or sunflower seed butter
- [ ] Wheat flour -> Chickpea flour, rice flour, buckwheat flour
- [ ] White sugar -> Dates, maple syrup, xylitol
- [ ] Cow's milk -> Coconut milk, almond milk, oat milk
- [ ] Yellow cheese -> Nutritional yeast, vegan cheese
- [ ] Eggs -> Flaxseed + water, chia + water, aquafaba
- [ ] Cream -> Coconut cream (thick part)
- [ ] Mayonnaise -> Vegan mayonnaise or tahini + lemon
- [ ] Butter -> Ghee, vegan butter, coconut oil

## Notes
Always read labels on "vegan" products - they may contain sugar and other unwanted ingredients.
