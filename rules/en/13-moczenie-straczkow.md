# Soaking Legumes
category: diet/preparation

## Rules
- [ ] Beans (all types): 10-12 hours
- [ ] Chickpeas: 8-12 hours
- [ ] Red lentils: 1-2 hours (optional)
- [ ] Green/brown lentils: 4-6 hours
- [ ] Mung beans: 4-8 hours
- [ ] Unroasted buckwheat groats: 2-4 hours
- [ ] Quinoa: rinse thoroughly (saponins!)
- [ ] Almonds: overnight, remove the skin

## Notes
Soaking reduces phytic acid, aids digestion, and shortens cooking time.
