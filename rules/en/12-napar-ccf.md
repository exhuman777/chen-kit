# Ayurvedic CCF Tea
category: health/ayurveda

## Rules
- [ ] C = Cumin: 1/2 teaspoon
- [ ] C = Coriander (seeds): 1/2 teaspoon
- [ ] F = Fennel: 1/2 teaspoon
- [ ] Pour boiling water over, steep for 10 minutes covered
- [ ] Drink warm, between meals

## Notes
Supports digestion, reduces bloating, balances all doshas. Can be consumed daily.
