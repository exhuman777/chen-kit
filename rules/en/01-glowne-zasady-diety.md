# Core Diet Rules
category: diet/core

## Restrictions
- [ ] BAN: The combination of Sugar + Flour + Fat (the "unholy trinity")
- [ ] Avoid white sugar and simple sugars
- [ ] Avoid wheat flour (especially gluten)
- [ ] Avoid peanut butter
- [ ] Cheese: avoid or eat very little
- [ ] Plant milk: use only conditionally (rarely)

## Rules
- [ ] Breakfast: Carbohydrates (porridges, oatmeal, rice porridge)
- [ ] Lunch: Vegetables + Plant-based protein (legumes, tofu, tempeh)
- [ ] Dinner: Light vegetables
- [ ] Honey: use only conditionally (rarely)
- [ ] Drink tea with mandarin peel

## Notes
These rules override ingredients in recipes. If a recipe contains peanut butter or wheat flour, look for substitutes.
