# Breakfast Rules
category: diet/meals

## Rules
- [ ] Carbohydrates as the main component
- [ ] Porridges: millet, buckwheat, barley
- [ ] Oatmeal: oat, rice, barley flakes
- [ ] Rice porridge: with warming spices
- [ ] Warm breakfast is better than cold
- [ ] Fruits: cooked or stewed (easier to digest)
- [ ] Spices: cinnamon, cardamom, ginger

## Restrictions
- [ ] Avoid cold cereal with cold milk
- [ ] Avoid raw fruits on an empty stomach (for some people)

## Notes
Morning Agni is weak - warm, light meals awaken it.
