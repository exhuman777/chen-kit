# Healing Soup Ingredients
category: health/tcm

## Rules
- [ ] Homemade, long-simmered broth as the base
- [ ] Fresh ginger: peeled and sliced
- [ ] A teaspoon of goji berries
- [ ] Red dates (Jujubes): 2 pieces, pitted (add at the beginning)
- [ ] Black sesame: for kidney strengthening
- [ ] TCM herbs after consultation: wild yam, longan fruit
- [ ] Turmeric: anti-inflammatory
- [ ] Red lentils: easily digestible

## Notes
Cook healing soups slowly, over low heat. Add red dates and goji at the beginning of cooking.
