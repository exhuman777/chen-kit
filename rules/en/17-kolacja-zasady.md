# Dinner Rules
category: diet/meals

## Rules
- [ ] Light vegetables as the main component
- [ ] Vegetable soups: ideal for the evening
- [ ] Cooked/steamed vegetables are better than raw
- [ ] Eat a minimum of 2-3 hours before bedtime
- [ ] Avoid heavy proteins in the evening
- [ ] Smaller portions than lunch

## Restrictions
- [ ] Avoid raw salads in the evening (hard to digest)
- [ ] Avoid heavy desserts
- [ ] Do not eat right before bedtime

## Notes
Evening Agni is weak. A heavy meal = indigestion, poor sleep.
