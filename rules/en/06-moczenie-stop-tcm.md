# Foot Soaking (TCM)
category: health/tcm

## Rules
- [ ] Soak feet in water at hot bath temperature
- [ ] Duration: 20-30 minutes
- [ ] Timing: One hour before bedtime
- [ ] Ginger powder addition: for women's health, fertility, feeling cold
- [ ] Black vinegar addition: for sweating, menopause, hot flashes

## Notes
Supports Dang Gui (Chinese Angelica) - "ginseng for women" for cycle regulation and PMS.
