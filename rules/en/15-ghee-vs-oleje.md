# Ghee and Oils
category: diet/fats

## Rules
- [ ] Ghee: best for frying, sattvic, supports Agni
- [ ] Coconut oil: for baking, cooling
- [ ] Sesame oil: warming, for massage and cooking
- [ ] Olive oil: cold use only, for salads
- [ ] Flaxseed oil: cold use only, omega-3
- [ ] Cold-pressed rapeseed oil: all-purpose

## Restrictions
- [ ] Do not overheat olive oil
- [ ] Do not use flaxseed oil for frying

## Notes
Ghee contains no casein or lactose - safe for those with milk intolerance.
