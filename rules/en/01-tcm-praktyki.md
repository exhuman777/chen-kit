## Evening Rituals (Foot Soaking)
The secret to health according to TCM. Perform one hour before bedtime.
- [ ] Water at "hot-tub" temperature (hot).
- [ ] Duration: 20 to 30 minutes.
- [ ] **Ginger addition (powder):** For women's health, when trying to conceive, feeling cold, loose stools.
- [ ] **Black vinegar addition:** For easy sweating, feeling hot, menopause.

## Lungs (Yin Deficiency)
Symptoms: dryness, empty heat, sadness.
- [ ] **White vegetables:** Cauliflower (soup), turnip, radish (pan-fried in Ghee).
- [ ] **Compote:** Made from pears and almonds.
- [ ] **Beverage:** Homemade almond milk.
- [ ] **For sinus mucus:** Light broth from napa cabbage and soy sauce.

## Kidneys and Vitality
- [ ] **Adzuki beans:** Reduce swelling, strengthen kidneys/adrenal glands, clear damp heat, strengthen Qi/Blood/Yin.
- [ ] **Black sesame:** Cooked on an empty stomach or added to soups (strengthens kidneys).
- [ ] **Dang Gui:** "Ginseng for women" - cycle support, PMS relief (use cautiously/after consultation).

## Notes
TCM takes a holistic approach - organs are connected to emotions and seasons. Evening foot soaking is one of the simplest and most effective rituals.
