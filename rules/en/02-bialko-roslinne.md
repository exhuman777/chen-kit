# Plant-Based Protein Sources
category: diet/protein

## Rules
- [ ] Mung beans: Ideal for Kitchari, detoxifies heavy metals
- [ ] Adzuki beans: Supports kidneys (TCM), good for autumn/winter
- [ ] Red lentils: Quick, for Indian dishes (soak 1h)
- [ ] Black lentils: For kidney strengthening (soak overnight)
- [ ] Tempeh: Smoked, fried, or plain
- [ ] Almonds: Without skin, soaked overnight (Ayurvedic must have)
- [ ] Walnuts: For brain function
- [ ] Sesame: Seeds, tahini, gomasio
- [ ] Golden flaxseed: Ground or as a gel
- [ ] Spirulina and Chlorella: For anemia
- [ ] Buckwheat groats: Unroasted, soaked
- [ ] Quinoa: Rinse thoroughly before cooking
- [ ] Tofu: Various types (plain, smoked)

## Notes
Remember to soak legumes (10-12h for beans) and rinse Quinoa.
